### Data Files

- **data_arg_n.rds**  
  Original dataset from the online survey conducted in Argentina using a quota sample designed to approximate national representation (September–October 2024).

- **Codebook_OnlineSurvey_Argentina.pdf**  
  Codebook including variable descriptions, question wording, and response categories from the online survey conducted in Argentina using a quota sample designed to approximate national representation (September–October 2024).

**data_arg_benef.rds**
Original ataset from the face-to-face survey conducted in Buenos Aires City during October–December 2024 with a convenient sample of AUH beneficiaries.
